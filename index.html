Welcome to Pacific Trails Resort

Relax, explore, and enjoy the coast at your own pace.

Pacific Trails Resort offers a peaceful coastal escape surrounded by scenic hiking trails, ocean views, and relaxing accommodations. Guests can enjoy guided nature walks, kayaking, and quiet evenings by the shore. Whether you are seeking adventure or rest, Pacific Trails provides the perfect setting for a memorable getaway.
